Visit Coolidge

Enjoy a small hometown out in the country.

Top three activities to do at Coolidge

Coolidge Days

Every year in October we have Coolidge Days. There are carnival games, a ferris wheel, and great food!

Fly In

Every first Saturday of the month, there is a fly in at the Coolidge Airport. Breakfast is served.

Pita Patio

The best resturant in town! They have live music and greek/american food. There's something for everyone!
